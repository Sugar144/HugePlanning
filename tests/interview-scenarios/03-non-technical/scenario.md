# Scenario 03 — non-technical (solution decomposition)

**Client (fictitious):** "Peluquería Loli" — hairdresser, owner Loli
(answers almost exclusively in proposed solutions heard from relatives and
competitors; zero technical vocabulary). **Archetype:** booking-system
hypothesis · **Profile:** standard · **Language:** es · **Protocol:** import
mode (TASK-018) — ingest `import-material.md`, gap analysis, follow-ups
answered from the persona.

**Run:** scratch client at stage `discovery`, G0 recorded. This scenario is
the 04 §3 worked example (R2-25) expanded: nearly every wish arrives wrapped
in an implementation.

## Persona facts (behind the proposals; reveal only when the agent digs)

- "Mi cuñado dice que lo hagamos con Wix" → real need: cheap, low-
  maintenance, "que no haya que tocar nada".
- "Quiero un botón de WhatsApp gigante" → need: clients book by message
  because they call while she has scissors in hand; message beats call.
- "Como la peluquería de enfrente, con la ruedecita para elegir hora" →
  need: clients see free slots; Loli hates the back-and-forth. But: she
  also wants final say ("a algunas clientas no las pongo con cualquiera")
  — auto-booking vs approval tension worth surfacing.
- "Casi nadie cancela, eso da igual" → exception probe: when someone DOES
  cancel, the slot stays lost — actual pain, she just normalized it.
- "Que salga la primera en Google" → need: findability; expectation to be
  managed as preference/OQ, not promised.
- Facts when asked directly: 2 chairs + 1 assistant; services with fixed
  prices (list exists on paper); busy Saturdays; data = name + phone;
  budget "no sé, ¿mil?"; no deadline; her niece "sabe de internet" (the
  maintenance probe should establish reality: niece updates Instagram
  sometimes — skill level low).

## Planted material inventory

- 4+ proposed solutions to decompose (Wix, WhatsApp button, slot picker,
  Google first).
- 1 inference trap ("casi nadie cancela").
- 1 tension to surface (auto-booking vs personal assignment) — CTR or
  decision-pending, either honest form accepted.
- Vague preference: "moderna pero acogedora" (gate: one attempt → precision
  low).
- Spanish throughout — DEC-14: interview and playbacks in es.

## What the run must produce

State whose candidates are needs (with the proposals recorded as
preferences with stakeholder + weight), the cancellation assumption
registered and probed, maintenance skill recorded as observed reality, no
technical vocabulary in any question asked.
