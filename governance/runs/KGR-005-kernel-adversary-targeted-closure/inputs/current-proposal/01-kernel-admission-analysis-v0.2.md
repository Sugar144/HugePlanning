## 3. Admission-test application to expanded meaning

Each expanded meaning passes the KGR-002 constitutional admission discipline:

1. **Essential asset:** authority, intent, evidence, legitimacy, sensitive effects, liveness, or recoverability.
2. **Grave/systemic hazard:** each change closes a CRITICAL/HIGH/MEDIUM adversarial path or prevents a documented regression.
3. **Broad applicability:** wording applies across humans, agents, tools, providers, stages, and effect classes.
4. **Technology independence:** no provider, model, command, repository layout, threshold, or implementation mechanism is prescribed.
5. **Systemic consequence:** failure can create unauthorized effects, false acceptance, self-certification, permanent emergency governance, or abandoned state.
6. **Insufficient lower-layer protection:** without the constitutional boundary, a lower layer could decide its own applicability or invert the intended protection.
7. **Evaluable in principle:** authority lineage, effect lineage, evaluation-control provenance, claim lineage, state ownership, and adoption coverage can be assessed later.
8. **Operational detail excluded:** identities, schemas, thresholds, UI, retention periods, fixtures, and provider adapters remain routed below.
9. **Cost justified:** revisions close concrete exploit paths while preserving harmless reads, low-criticality consolidation, and proportionate evidence.
10. **Coherence:** all changes preserve K-001 floors, K-006 proportionality, and K-007 safe stopping without forced progress.

## 4. Architecture and preserved meanings

Preserved without material change:

- HugePlanning level-3 scope and explicit downstream adoption boundary.
- Human-only ratification and amendment.
- Authority hierarchy and ordinary-instruction non-amendment rule.
- Functional roles rather than mandatory permanent agents.
- Honest non-success states and prohibition on inferred acceptance.
- Technology-independent constitutional meaning.
- Anti-fragmentation, retrospective honesty, interruption handling, and seven-clause structure.

Materially revised:

- fixed human C3/C4 and high-risk acceptance floor;
- governed-effect semantics beyond state mutation;
- indirect evaluation-chain control;
- purpose-to-claim and claim-to-gate traceability;
- privacy/security-compatible evidentiary integrity;
- cumulative exception/emergency duration and effect;
- accountable blocked-state lifecycle;
- scoped enforceability coverage;
- non-recursive complexity justification;
- informed reserved human acceptance;
- material-assumption currency; and
- bounded fundamental-architecture reservation.
