# KGR-005 Targeted Adversarial Scenarios

## Required scenario results

- Target: KA-F-012; K-004, K-007.
- Setup and attack: a material assumption remains labeled but has no effective currentness and supports dependent work indefinitely.
- Expected protection: owner plus validity/revalidation condition; stale or contradicted assumptions block dependent gates.
- Observed constitutional result: K-004 makes both requirements operative and K-007 keeps the resulting dependency state owned and reviewable.
- Residual risk: registries and triggers are not implemented.
- Verdict: `PASS`.

### KA-S-002 — Indirect criterion weakening through fixtures

- Target: KA-F-003; K-003, K-004, K-005.

- Expected protection: fixture/evidence control counts as decisive-chain control and invalidates the result.
- Observed constitutional result: K-003 expressly covers fixtures, evidence sets, criteria, and indirect weakening; its violation effect returns the result to a non-accepted state.
- Residual risk: provenance controls remain downstream.
- Verdict: `PASS`.
