## 4. Principal semantic changes

- **K-001:** human final acceptance for C3/C4 and acceptance of serious/critical/constitutional residual risk are unconditional constitutional reservations; lower layers cannot decide the gate is unnecessary. Fundamental architecture is reserved only when its material effects change constitutional properties.
- **K-002:** authorization covers all governed effects, including consequential read-only access/transmission, material cost, external exposure, and authoritative outputs.
- **K-003:** independence covers control of the decisive evaluation chain, not evaluator identity alone.
- **K-004:** canonical authority may be composite with explicit boundaries; material assumptions require currency; summaries require fidelity; raw evidence may receive competent documented least-loss handling.
- **K-005:** gate claims bind to canonical objective, criteria, scope, and dependencies; narrow PASS stays narrow; reserved human acceptance requires an attributable comprehensible decision basis; recurring limitations aggregate.
- **K-006:** aggregation spans actors, branches, stages, releases, systems, and time; burden justification is material and non-recursive.
- **K-007:** safe stopping includes significant non-state effects, accountable blocked-state lifecycle, cumulative emergency disposition, and evidence-based distinction between restoration and compensation.
- **Adoption:** ENFORCEABLE is scoped and requires demonstrated applicable constitutional control coverage, not one mechanism.
