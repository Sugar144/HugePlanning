# Change Control

## Policy

Agents may draft and propose changes but never approve them. An `approved`
artifact is immutable except through the applicable gate/change procedure.
Preserve prior IDs and records; use terminal status plus `superseded_by` instead
of rewriting history. Gate decisions are human, explicit, and recorded.

## Why

Approved scope and decisions are commercial and technical baselines, not mutable
working notes.

## Observable violation

An agent approval, silent edit to approved meaning, deleted historical decision,
reused ID, unrecorded gate decision, or implementation started before required
approval.
